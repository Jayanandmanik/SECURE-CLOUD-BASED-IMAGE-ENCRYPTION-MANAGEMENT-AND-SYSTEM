What is AES-256 Encryption?

AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard) is a symmetric encryption algorithm widely used for securing sensitive data. It uses a 256-bit key, making it one of the most secure encryption methods available today. Whether you're encrypting text or images, AES-256 ensures your data remains confidential and tamper-proof.
